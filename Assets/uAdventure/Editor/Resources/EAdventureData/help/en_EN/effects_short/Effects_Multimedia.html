Effects multimedia

The multimedia effects allow us to launch sound, animations or videos (those last one from the cutscenes).

The effects belonging to this group are:

  • Play sound: allows to choose a sound to play it. Most common formats are MP3 and MIDI.
  • Play animation: allow a certain animation to be played either for some of the characters or for the player.
  • Launch cutscene: launch a cutscene. There is a kind of cutscenes known as video scenes (because they reproduce a video)
