Move Player effect

Through this effect we can move the player to the selected position.

Create a move player effect:

  • Select the effect: Select the effect on the creation effects panel. Press OK.
  • Creation effect panel: afterwards the next panel will appear:

In the first place we are allowed to select one scene of the chapter. Like in other effects, the selection of the scene has the only aim of previewing the scene, because the effect will be triggered in the current scene. The player will move with independence of the scene selected in this panel, depending only on the scene of the game where the effect is triggered. Once the scene is selected, we choose the position where the player will move, either fulfilling X and Y coordinates or just by clicking in the position selected in the scene.
