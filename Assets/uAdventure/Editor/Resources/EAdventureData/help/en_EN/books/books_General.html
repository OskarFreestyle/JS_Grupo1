Books

Books are used in games to show information to the player. There are two types of books:

  • Simple content: If this option is chosen, the book is build via attaching paragraphs with plain text or images. Other facilities supported are simple bullets and titles (text with a larger font size)
  • Formatted text: This type of book supports richer content: formatted text in RTF or HTML format. An editor for this kinds of books is included.
